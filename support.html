Frequently asked questions

Where is my data stored?

Keepling is local-first — your moments, photos and voice notes are stored privately on your device. No account is required. With Keepling Premium you can optionally back up to your own Google Drive.

What does Keepling Premium include?

Automatic Google Drive backup, an ad-free experience, unlimited photos and voice notes, and growth-documentary export. It's an auto-renewing monthly subscription that you can cancel anytime in your device Settings.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone → tap your name → Subscriptions → Keepling, where you can change or cancel your plan.

How do I move my data to a new phone?

With Premium, enable Google Drive backup on your old device, then sign in with the same Google account on the new one and restore from Settings → Backup.

How do I delete my data?

Deleting a child profile removes its moments from the device. Removing the app deletes all local data. Any Google Drive backup can be deleted from your own Drive.
