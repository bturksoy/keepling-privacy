# Keepling — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 14 June 2026_

Keepling ("the app", "we", "us") helps parents keep small moments of their child
growing up. We designed Keepling to be **private by default**. This policy
explains what data the app handles and how.

## 1. Who the app is for

Keepling is intended for use by **parents and guardians (adults)**. It is not
directed to children and is not intended to be used by children. The app may
contain information that a parent chooses to record about their child; that
content is controlled by the parent (see below).

## 2. Data stored on your device

Everything you create in Keepling — your child's name and date of birth, notes,
photos, voice recordings, moods and milestones — is stored **locally on your
device**. We do not operate servers that receive or store this content, and we
cannot see it.

## 3. Google Drive backup (optional)

If you choose to connect Google Drive (a premium feature), Keepling creates a
single backup file and stores it in your Drive's hidden, app-private
`appDataFolder`. We request only the `drive.appdata` permission, which limits the
app to this private folder — Keepling **cannot read the rest of your Google
Drive**. The backup lives in _your_ Google account, not ours. You can delete it
at any time from your Google account's app-data settings.

## 4. Advertising (free version)

The free version shows ads via **Google AdMob**. To serve ads, Google and its
partners may collect and use a device advertising identifier and related data.
Please review [Google's advertising policy](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads).
Where required (e.g. in the EEA/UK), you will be asked for consent. Subscribing
to Keepling Premium removes ads.

## 5. Purchases

Keepling Premium is sold as a subscription processed by **Google Play Billing**.
We do not receive or store your payment details; payment is handled by Google.

## 6. Device permissions

- **Camera / Photos** — only to let you add photos to a moment. Photos stay on your device.
- **Microphone** — only to let you record short voice notes. Recordings stay on your device.
- **Internet** — used for ads (free version) and Google Drive backup (if enabled).

## 7. Sharing & export

You can export a backup or a PDF album using your device's share sheet. When you
do, you choose the destination (Drive, Files, email, etc.). That action is
entirely under your control.

## 8. Data retention & deletion

Uninstalling Keepling removes all data stored on your device. To remove a Google
Drive backup, delete the app data from your Google account
([drive.google.com](https://drive.google.com) → Settings → Manage apps), or use
the in-app disconnect option.

## 9. Children's data

Any information about a child that a parent records is treated as the parent's
personal content: stored locally and, if enabled, in the parent's own Google
Drive. We do not collect it, sell it, or share it.

## 10. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last
updated" date above.
